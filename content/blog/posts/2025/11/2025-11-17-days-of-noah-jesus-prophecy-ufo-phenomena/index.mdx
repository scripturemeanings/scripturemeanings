*When Jesus warned "as it was in the days of Noah," He wasn't making a casual comparison—He was pointing to a specific pattern that would repeat before His return.*

---

**Matthew 24:37-39:**

> "For as were the days of Noah, so will be the coming of the Son of Man. For as in those days before the flood they were eating and drinking, marrying and giving in marriage, until the day when Noah entered the ark, and they were unaware until the flood came and swept them all away, so will be the coming of the Son of Man."

Most Bible teachers interpret this passage as a warning about complacency—people living normal lives, unaware of coming judgment. And that's true.

But it's not the whole truth.

When Jesus said "as in the days of Noah," He was specifically referencing Genesis 6—a chapter that describes far more than people eating and drinking. It describes the Watchers descending, genetic corruption, the Nephilim, forbidden knowledge, violence filling the earth, and God's judgment through the Flood.

Jesus wasn't just warning about spiritual apathy. He was warning that the specific conditions of Genesis 6 would repeat before His return:

- Non-human entities interacting with humanity

- Genetic manipulation and hybrid beings

- Forbidden knowledge being revealed

- Corruption of human DNA

- Violence and lawlessness increasing

- The earth being filled with wickedness

And when we look at modern UFO phenomena—"aliens" conducting breeding programs, creating hybrids, promising advanced knowledge, working toward "disclosure"—we see Genesis 6 repeating exactly as Jesus prophesied.

This isn't coincidence. This is prophecy being fulfilled in our generation.

Understanding why Jesus pointed to Noah's day helps us recognize what we're witnessing now and what's coming next.

## What Made Noah's Days Unique?

To understand Jesus' prophecy, we must first understand what made Noah's generation unique in all of human history.

### The Genesis 6 Account

**Genesis 6:1-4:**

> "When man began to multiply on the face of the land and daughters were born to them, the sons of God saw that the daughters of man were attractive. And they took as their wives any they chose. Then the LORD said, 'My Spirit shall not abide in man forever, for he is flesh: his days shall be 120 years.' The Nephilim were on the earth in those days, and also afterward, when the sons of God came in to the daughters of man and they bore children to them. These were the mighty men who were of old, the men of renown."

**Key elements:**

**"Sons of God" (bene elohim)** - In Old Testament usage, this term consistently refers to angelic beings (Job 1:6, 2:1, 38:7). The Watchers, fallen angels, descended to Earth.

**"Took wives"** - Not metaphorical. Physical, reproductive relationships producing offspring.

**"Nephilim"** - Hybrid beings, part angelic/part human. Giants. The product of forbidden union between spiritual beings and humans.

**"Mighty men of old, men of renown"** - These weren't myths. They were real beings whose exploits became the basis for ancient legends of demigods and heroes.

### The Corruption That Required Judgment

**Genesis 6:5:**

> "The LORD saw that the wickedness of man was great in the earth, and that every intention of the thoughts of his heart was only evil continually."

**Genesis 6:11-12:**

> "Now the earth was corrupt in God's sight, and the earth was filled with violence. And God saw the earth, and behold, it was corrupt, for all flesh had corrupted their way on the earth."

**"All flesh had corrupted their way"** - The Hebrew *kol basar* includes humans and animals. The corruption wasn't just moral—it was genetic.

The Watchers' program didn't stop with human-angelic hybrids. It spread to animals (Genesis 6:12), creating chimeras, corrupting God's created order at the DNA level.

This is why God needed a global flood. Local judgment wouldn't eliminate genetic corruption that had spread throughout creation.

### Why Noah Was Chosen

**Genesis 6:8-9:**

> "But Noah found favor in the eyes of the LORD... Noah was a righteous man, blameless in his generation. Noah walked with God."

**"Blameless in his generation"** - The Hebrew *tamim* can mean morally blameless, but in context also suggests genetic purity. Noah's lineage was uncorrupted—his DNA wasn't mixed with Nephilim genetics.

**Genesis 7:1:**

> "Then the LORD said to Noah, 'Go into the ark, you and all your household, for I have seen that you are righteous before me in this generation.'"

God preserved Noah because he was genetically pure, enabling the continuation of uncompromised humanity after the Flood destroyed the corrupted hybrid populations.

### The Violence and Wickedness

**Genesis 6:13:**

> "And God said to Noah, 'I have determined to make an end of all flesh, for the earth is filled with violence through them. Behold, I will destroy them with the earth.'"

The Nephilim and their descendants created unprecedented violence. As hybrids with superhuman strength and demonic influence, they dominated and oppressed humanity.

Ancient texts like 1 Enoch describe them consuming human flesh, drinking blood, teaching warfare and weapons—creating a civilization of terror and wickedness that required divine intervention.

## Jesus' Specific Warning: The Pattern Will Repeat

When Jesus said "as in the days of Noah," He was making a precise prophetic statement: the conditions of Genesis 6 will return before His second coming.

### The Full Context of Jesus' Warning

**Matthew 24:36-39:**

> "But concerning that day and hour no one knows, not even the angels of heaven, nor the Son, but the Father only. For as were the days of Noah, so will be the coming of the Son of Man. For as in those days before the flood they were eating and drinking, marrying and giving in marriage, until the day when Noah entered the ark, and they were unaware until the flood came and swept them all away, so will be the coming of the Son of Man."

**The context:** Jesus is discussing His return and end-times signs. He's telling His disciples what to watch for.

**The comparison:** "As were the days of Noah" - using the definite article "the" indicates He's referencing specific, known conditions, not just general complacency.

**The Jewish understanding:** First-century Jews knew Genesis 6 thoroughly. They understood the Watchers, the Nephilim, the genetic corruption. When Jesus referenced Noah's days, they knew exactly what He meant.

### What Jesus' Jewish Audience Understood

When Jesus spoke these words, His Jewish audience had access to texts that expanded on Genesis 6:

**The Book of Enoch** - Widely read in Second Temple Judaism, describing the Watchers' descent, the genetic program, the Nephilim's violence, and God's judgment.

**The Book of Jubilees** - Providing chronology and details of the Genesis 6 event.

**The Genesis Apocryphon** - Found at Qumran, describing Noah's unusual appearance at birth because genetic corruption was so widespread.

**The Book of Giants** - Describing the Nephilim from their own perspective, their dreams of coming judgment, their knowledge they were unnatural beings.

**Jesus' audience didn't need explanation.** They knew "days of Noah" meant:

- Watchers descending/interacting with humans

- Hybrid beings produced

- Genetic corruption spreading

- Forbidden knowledge revealed

- Violence and wickedness increasing

- Divine judgment becoming necessary

### What Must Repeat Before Christ Returns

If "as in the days of Noah" is literal prophecy, then these specific conditions must return:

**1. Non-human Entities Interacting with Humanity**

The Watchers descended in Genesis 6. Before Christ returns, non-human entities will again openly interact with humans.

**Modern fulfillment:** "Alien" contact increasing, government "disclosure" preparing humanity for open interaction, entities presenting themselves as advanced beings from other planets.

**2. Genetic Manipulation and Hybrid Creation**

The Watchers produced Nephilim through genetic mixing. Before Christ returns, genetic manipulation creating hybrid beings will occur again.

**Modern fulfillment:** "Alien" abduction reports consistently describing breeding programs, hybrid children, multi-generational refinement toward beings that can "integrate" into human society.

**3. Corruption of Human DNA**

All flesh corrupted its way. Before Christ returns, human DNA will again be altered/corrupted.

**Modern fulfillment:** CRISPR gene editing, mRNA technology, transhumanism, human-animal chimeras, genetic "enhancement," push to upgrade humanity beyond natural limits.

**4. Forbidden Knowledge Revealed**

The Watchers taught forbidden arts—weapons, sorcery, genetic manipulation, astronomy. Before Christ returns, knowledge humans shouldn't possess will be revealed again.

**Modern fulfillment:** "Aliens" promising advanced technology, scientific breakthroughs, solutions to climate/disease/war, "evolution to next level"—knowledge that leads away from God.

**5. Increase of Violence and Wickedness**

The earth was filled with violence. Before Christ returns, violence and wickedness will increase dramatically.

**Modern fulfillment:** Global violence increasing, persecution of Christians intensifying, lawlessness rising, moral standards collapsing—leading toward Tribulation conditions.

**6. Humanity Unaware Until Too Late**

They were unaware until the Flood came. Before Christ returns, most of humanity will be oblivious to the spiritual reality until judgment arrives.

**Modern fulfillment:** The "alien gospel" deceiving masses, strong delusion preparing people to accept Antichrist, few recognizing the spiritual nature of what's occurring.

## The "Days of Noah" Pattern in Biblical Prophecy

Jesus' reference to Noah isn't isolated. Other New Testament passages confirm the pattern will repeat.

### Luke's Parallel Account

**Luke 17:26-30:**

> "Just as it was in the days of Noah, so will it be in the days of the Son of Man. They were eating and drinking and marrying and being given in marriage, until the day when Noah entered the ark, and the flood came and destroyed them all. Likewise, just as it was in the days of Lot—they were eating and drinking, buying and selling, planting and building, but on the day when Lot went out from Sodom, fire and sulfur rained from heaven and destroyed them all—so will it be on the day when the Son of Man is revealed."

Luke adds Lot's day to Noah's day, creating a dual comparison:

**Noah's day:** Genetic corruption, Watchers, Nephilim, violence

**Lot's day:** Sexual immorality, Sodom, angels visiting (Genesis 19), supernatural judgment

Both involved:

- Supernatural beings interacting with humans

- Sexual sin (Watchers with women, Sodomites wanting angels)

- Complete moral corruption

- Sudden divine judgment

- Few saved (8 in ark, 4 escaped Sodom—3 ultimately)

The pattern: supernatural beings involved in sexual corruption, leading to divine judgment.

### Peter's Confirmation

**2 Peter 2:4-6:**

> "For if God did not spare angels when they sinned, but cast them into hell and committed them to chains of gloomy darkness to be kept until the judgment; if he did not spare the ancient world, but preserved Noah, a herald of righteousness, with seven others, when he brought a flood upon the world of the ungodly; if by turning the cities of Sodom and Gomorrah to ashes he condemned them to extinction, making them an example of what is going to happen to the ungodly..."

Peter explicitly connects:

- Angels sinning (Genesis 6, the Watchers)

- Noah's flood (judgment on that corruption)

- Sodom's destruction (sexual corruption involving angels)

- Future judgment of the ungodly (pattern repeating)

**Verse 4:** "Angels when they sinned" - Peter confirms Genesis 6 involved angelic sin, not just human wickedness.

**Verse 5:** The flood was specifically judgment on the corruption those angels caused.

**Verse 6:** Sodom is parallel—another instance of supernatural-human sexual corruption requiring judgment.

**"Making them an example"** - These past judgments are templates for what's coming. The same corruption will require the same response: divine judgment.

### Jude's Warning

**Jude 6-7:**

> "And the angels who did not stay within their own position of authority, but left their proper dwelling, he has kept in eternal chains under gloomy darkness until the judgment of the great day—just as Sodom and Gomorrah and the surrounding cities, which likewise indulged in sexual immorality and pursued unnatural desire, serve as an example by undergoing a punishment of eternal fire."

**"Left their proper dwelling"** - Angels abandoned their assigned realm (heaven) to interact physically with humans (Genesis 6).

**"Just as Sodom"** - The comparison is explicit. Sodom's sin was "likewise"—parallel to the angels' sin. The men of Sodom wanted sexual relations with angels (Genesis 19:5), mirroring the Watchers' sin with human women.

**"Unnatural desire"** - Greek *sarkos heteras* (strange flesh, different flesh). Sexual relations crossing the boundary between different kinds of beings—human and angelic.

**"Serve as an example"** - Past judgments warn of future judgment. The pattern established in Noah's day and Lot's day will repeat, requiring similar divine response.

## Modern Fulfillment: UFO Phenomena as Genesis 6 Repeating

When we examine modern UFO phenomena through the lens of Jesus' prophecy, the parallels to Genesis 6 are undeniable.

### Parallel #1: Non-Human Entities Interacting with Humans

**Genesis 6:** Watchers descended from heaven, took human form, interacted directly with humanity.

**Modern:** "Aliens" appear, abduct humans, communicate telepathically, make promises, present themselves as guides for humanity's evolution.

Both involve non-human intelligent beings claiming to assist humanity while actually corrupting it.

### Parallel #2: Reproductive/Genetic Focus

**Genesis 6:** The entire program centered on reproduction—Watchers taking wives, producing offspring, creating hybrids.

**Modern:** UFO abduction reports overwhelmingly involve reproductive procedures: egg/sperm collection, forced breeding, hybrid offspring shown to abductees, multi-generational breeding programs.

Both involve systematic genetic manipulation to produce hybrid beings.

### Parallel #3: Progressive Hybrid Generations

**Genesis 6:** First generation Nephilim were giants. Second generation (Elioud, born from Nephilim, mentioned in apocryphal texts) more human-like. Progressive refinement toward beings that could integrate into society.

**Modern:** UFO researchers document progressive hybrid generations: F1 (greys, obviously non-human) → F2 → F3 → F4 → F5 (hubrids, almost fully human appearance). Same pattern: refining toward beings that can live undetected among humans.

Both involve multi-generational breeding programs working toward integration.

### Parallel #4: Forbidden Knowledge Transfer

**Genesis 6:** Each Watcher taught specific forbidden knowledge: weapons, sorcery, astronomy, cosmetics, genetic manipulation ("root-cutting"), metallurgy.

**Modern:** Contactees report "aliens" providing advanced technology, scientific knowledge, occult abilities, consciousness expansion, cosmic secrets—always leading away from biblical truth toward New Age philosophy.

Both involve knowledge transfer that leads humans away from God.

### Parallel #5: Deception as Enlightenment

**Genesis 6:** The Watchers presented their program as beneficial—teaching humanity, elevating consciousness, revealing secrets. But it led to corruption requiring judgment.

**Modern:** "Aliens" present as benevolent guides helping humanity evolve to the next level, but their message systematically attacks Christ, biblical truth, and leads toward the strong delusion and Antichrist.

Both involve deception presented as enlightenment.

### Parallel #6: The Goal of Integration

**Genesis 6:** The Nephilim and their descendants sought to integrate into human society, dominate it, corrupt it completely. By Noah's time, only one family line remained genetically pure.

**Modern:** UFO researchers like Dr. David Jacobs document "The Change"—a planned event where fully integrated hybrids will openly take roles in human society, leading humanity into "the next phase." The goal: replacement and control.

Both involve replacing/controlling humanity through hybrid integration.

### Parallel #7: Increasing Toward Climax

**Genesis 6:** The corruption intensified progressively until God could no longer allow it to continue. The tipping point was reached, requiring global judgment.

**Modern:** UFO disclosure accelerating, government acknowledgment, mainstream acceptance, preparation for "contact"—all building toward a climactic "revelation" that will deceive the world and usher in Antichrist.

Both involve progressive buildup toward a tipping point.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Doesn't "as in the days of Noah" just mean people will be living normal lives, unaware of judgment?**

A: That's part of it, but not all. Jesus specifically referenced "the days of Noah," not just "people living normal lives." His Jewish audience understood this as referencing the entire Genesis 6 context: Watchers, Nephilim, genetic corruption, violence, judgment. The normalcy is the final stage—corruption had become so normalized that only Noah recognized it. Both elements are part of the prophecy.

**Q: Could the "days of Noah" be symbolic/spiritual rather than literal?**

A: Jesus treated Noah as historical (Matthew 24:37-39), Peter treated the Flood as literal history (2 Peter 2:5, 3:6), and Hebrews lists Noah among faith heroes (Hebrews 11:7). If Genesis 6 is historical, Jesus' prophecy that those conditions will return is also literal. The specificity of the comparison argues against purely symbolic interpretation.

**Q: When will the "days of Noah" pattern be complete?**

A: We're watching it unfold now with UFO disclosure accelerating and genetic technology advancing. Full manifestation likely occurs during the Tribulation when "alien" entities openly appear, the Antichrist is revealed, and genetic corruption (mark of the beast?) becomes widespread. The pattern completes at Christ's return when He judges all corruption just as the Flood did.

**Q: Does this mean the Rapture is about to happen?**

A: The "days of Noah" pattern suggests we're in the season, but Jesus said "concerning that day and hour no one knows" (Matthew 24:36). We can recognize the season without date-setting. Noah entered the ark before the Flood; believers will be preserved before/through God's wrath. Whether that's pre-trib Rapture or another form of protection, God will preserve His people.

**Q: Should Christians study UFO phenomena to understand the prophecy better?**

A: Study biblical prophecy first and foremost. Understanding Genesis 6, the Book of Enoch, and end-times passages provides the framework. Then, limited study of UFO phenomena through a biblical lens can help recognize how the deception operates. But don't become obsessed—maintain primary focus on Scripture, not the deception itself.

**Q: What should I tell Christians who think UFOs are legitimate extraterrestrials?**

A: Ask: "Do these beings' messages align with Scripture? Do they confess Jesus Christ came in the flesh? (1 John 4:1-3) Do they point to Christ or away from Him?" Show how the "alien gospel" systematically contradicts biblical truth. Point to the Genesis 6 pattern and Jesus' prophecy. Help them test all things against Scripture (1 Thessalonians 5:21).
